Welcome. This is my corner of the internet — part notebook, part portfolio.

I'll be writing about the things I tinker with: **robotics**, **tinyML** (machine
learning on tiny hardware), **American football analytics**, and **data analysis**
more broadly. Expect build logs, half-finished experiments, and the occasional
chart that took way too long to make.

## What you'll find here

- **[Projects]({{ '/projects/' | relative_url }})** — longer-form write-ups of things I'm building.
- **[Blog]({{ '/blog/' | relative_url }})** — shorter notes, experiments, and articles.
- **[About]({{ '/' | relative_url }})** — who I am and how to reach me.

## Writing here is easy

Posts are just Markdown files in `_posts/`, named `YYYY-MM-DD-title.md`. This theme
supports a lot out of the box — math, syntax-highlighted code, interactive charts,
diagrams, and even embedded Jupyter notebooks — so I can drop real analysis straight
into a post.
